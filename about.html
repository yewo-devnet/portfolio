About Me

My name is Yewo Mkandawire, a passionate and driven third-year Computer Network Engineering student at the University of Malawi (UNIMA)—the country's top institution. With a strong foundation in programming, networking, and system administration, Adept at problem-solving and implementing efficient solutions, from configuring routing protocols and optimizing database performance to developing user-centric web applications. Today, that same curiosity drives me to explore cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and embedded programming to create practical applications that can transform lives and communities.
